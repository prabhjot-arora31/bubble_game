Bubble Game

Developer: PRABHJOT SINGH ARORA

Score: 10


Instructions

  • This is a simple Web based Game.
  • After starting the game , bubbles will be displayed at center and target will be displayed at top.
  • Game is to click the Target number in the bubbles to score well.
  • After clicking the Target Number bubble, Target as well as Numbers in the bubble will change.
  • After starting the game, the game will not stop.
  • The Score will be displayed at top right and after the game is over
